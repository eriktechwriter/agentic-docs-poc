title: Mark vulnerability risk events as dismissed, accepted, or remediated
---

June 17, 2024—As with risk events in other risk factors, you may now mark events in the vulnerabilities risk factor as remediated, dismissed, or accepted. The new workflow helps streamline the process of managing risk events and CVEs. To learn more about the statuses, see [Risk assessment](risk-assessment.md).
