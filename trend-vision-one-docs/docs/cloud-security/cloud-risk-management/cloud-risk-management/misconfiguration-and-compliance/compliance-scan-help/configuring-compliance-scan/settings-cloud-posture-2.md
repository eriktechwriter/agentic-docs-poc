The Compliance scan, which can be customized to your specific needs, performs several checks to ensure that your AWS account complies with the AWS Well-Architected Framework.

### Steps to update Compliance scan settings {#steps-to-update-compliance-scan-settings}

1.  From the Account Settings\>Compliance scan Settings
2.  Click on *Update Bot Settings*
3.  On the new pop-up window, you can view/edit the following fields
    - [Disable Cloud Risk Management Bot](../../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/compliance-scan-help/configuring-compliance-scan/disable)
    - [Compliance scan enabled regions](../../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/compliance-scan-help/configuring-compliance-scan/enabled-regions)
    - [Compliance scan frequency](../../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/compliance-scan-help/configuring-compliance-scan/frequency)
