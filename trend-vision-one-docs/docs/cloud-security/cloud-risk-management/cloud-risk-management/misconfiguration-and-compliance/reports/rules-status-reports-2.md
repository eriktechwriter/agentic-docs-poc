Trend Vision One™ – Cloud Risk Management offers multiple reports of rules run and check success and failures. These include

- [All Checks Report](../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/reports/all-checks-report) - current state of your Account against all rules enabled and active on your account.
- [Configured Reports](../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/reports/configured-reports) - custom reports using [filters](../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/misconfiguration-and-compliance-rules/search/filter-and-search/filter-search) that can be saved and scheduled for recurrent generation.
- [Cloud Risk Management Report](../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/reports/cloud-posture-report) - all rules run against each account daily.
- [Compliance and Cloud Risk Management Reports](../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/compliance/compliance-reports) - report that check compliance with a specific [Compliance Standard or Framework](https://www.cloudconformity.com/conformity-rules/Compliance/).

Checks can be exported either by:

- [Generate and Download Report](../../../../../cyber-risk-exposure-management/security-posture-management/cloud-risk-management/misconfiguration-and-compliance/reports/generate-download-report), or
- Cloud Risk Management [API for Checks](https://automation.trendmicro.com/cloudone/conformity/)
