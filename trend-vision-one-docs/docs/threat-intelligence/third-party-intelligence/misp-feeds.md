Trend Vision One enables transfer of suspicious object data and retrieval of threat intelligence data either through a Service Gateway or directly with the MISP threat sharing platform.

- To enable the transfer of suspicious object data and retrieval of MISP threat intelligence data through a Service Gateway, see [MISP integration (via Service Gateway)](../../workflow-and-automation/third-party-integrations/misp-integration).

- To enable the direct transfer of suspicious object data and retrieval of MISP threat intelligence data, see [MISP integration (via direct connection)](../../workflow-and-automation/third-party-integrations/misp-integration-direct-connect).
