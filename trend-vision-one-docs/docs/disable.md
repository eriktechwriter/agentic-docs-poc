title: Disable Compliance scan
---

Compliance scan status is set to *ON* by default. However, you can disable the bot either temporarily or permanently.

:::note
Trend Vision One™ – Cloud Risk Management strongly recommends that you do not disable the bot as it may expose your account to risks.
:::

1.  If you want to disable the bot temporarily, enter the number of hours

2.  If you want to disable the bot permanently, select Disable the Compliance scan until further notice

3.  Click on *Disable* and *Update scan settings*

4.  Once disabled, the status of the bot will be displayed on both:

    1.  [Compliance scan settings page](settings-cloud-posture.md) and
    2.  [Settings page](cloud-acct-cloud-posture.md)
