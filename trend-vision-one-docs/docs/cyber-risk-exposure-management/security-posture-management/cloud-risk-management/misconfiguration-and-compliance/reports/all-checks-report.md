The All Checks report provides the success and failure of all Rules enabled and active on your Account, Group, or Organisation. These can be viewed by *Rule,* *Resource,* or [Compliance Standard](../compliance/compliance-reports) to prioritize [failed checks for resolution](../misconfiguration-and-compliance-rules/checks/failed-check-resolution/failed-check-resolution). You can also view all excluded resources in your account.

We also provide users with options to :

1.  Generate [Configured reports](./configured-reports)
2.  [Filter checks](../misconfiguration-and-compliance-rules/search/filter-and-search/filter-search)
3.  [Generate report](./generate-download-report)
4.  [Resolve the failures](../misconfiguration-and-compliance-rules/checks/failed-check-resolution/failed-check-resolution)
5.  On clicking the expand button
    1.  [Send rule to](../misconfiguration-and-compliance-rules/checks/send-rule)
    2.  [Configure rule](../misconfiguration-and-compliance-rules/configurations/rules-configuration)
    3.  [Suppress](../misconfiguration-and-compliance-rules/checks/rules-suppress-check)
    4.  Create tickets depending on the [communication channels](../communication-and-notification/communication-channels/communication-integrations) configured

### View Excluded Resources {#view-excluded-resources}

To view excluded resources within your account:

1.  Scroll down to the bottom of the *All Checks* report.
2.  Click on the *View list* link under *Important information*.
