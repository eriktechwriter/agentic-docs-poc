Trend Vision One™ – Cloud Risk Management offers multiple reports of rules run and check success and failures. These include

- [All Checks Report](./all-checks-report) - current state of your Account against all rules enabled and active on your account.
- [Configured Reports](./configured-reports) - custom reports using [filters](../misconfiguration-and-compliance-rules/search/filter-and-search/filter-search) that can be saved and scheduled for recurrent generation.
- [Cloud Risk Management Report](./cloud-posture-report) - all rules run against each account daily.
- [Compliance and Cloud Risk Management Reports](../compliance/compliance-reports) - report that check compliance with a specific [Compliance Standard or Framework](https://www.cloudconformity.com/conformity-rules/Compliance/).

Checks can be exported either by:

- [Generate and Download Report](./generate-download-report), or
- Cloud Risk Management [API for Checks](https://automation.trendmicro.com/cloudone/conformity/)
