Trend Vision One™ – Cloud Risk Management supports notifications sent via Email.

### Set up an Email channel {#set-up-an-email-channel}

1.  Click on *Create another Email channel*
2.  [Set automatic notifications](../communication-settings/toggle-automatic-notifications)
3.  [Configure Triggers](../communication-settings/communication-triggers)
4.  [Configure Users](../communication-settings/communication-recipients)
5.  [Copy communication settings](../communication-settings/copy-communication-settings)

:::note
Active users without an email will not appear on the recipient list.
:::
